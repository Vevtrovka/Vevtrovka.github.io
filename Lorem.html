Lorem

Lorem is the beginning of the famous filler phrase "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet". It's used in design to show what text could look like. The word comes from a scrambled Latin passage. It helps designers and clients focus on layout over content. Lorem has no real meaning by itself. It looks natural enough to mimic real paragraphs. You'll see it often in websites, posters, and mockups. It's the silent hero of early-stage design work.
