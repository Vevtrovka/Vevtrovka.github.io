Sit

"Sit" is another part of the standard filler phrase. It comes from Latin, meaning "may it be" or "it is". In this case, it serves no purpose except appearance. It helps give the sentence a natural rhythm. That makes the text look like real content blocks. Sit is never read literally by the viewer. It only exists to complete the visual flow. Yet, it's been printed in millions of designs worldwide.
