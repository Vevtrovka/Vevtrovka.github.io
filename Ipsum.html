Ipsum

Ipsum is the second word in the traditional placeholder text. It helps simulate paragraphs during design or mockups. The word itself has Latin roots. Designers don't care about its meaning—only its appearance. It works well because it avoids readable distraction. Ipsum plays a role in visualizing page structure. You'll find it in templates, presentations, and test websites. Like "Lorem", it’s part of every designer’s toolkit.
