Dolor

"Dolor" is Latin for "pain", but here it's just filler. It’s part of the famous dummy phrase "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet". Designers use it to block out space for real text. The goal is to test fonts, layout, and formatting. Dolor carries no emotion or meaning in this use. It prevents people from focusing on the wrong things early. It’s repeated countless times in design history. Even though it's old, it's still useful today.
